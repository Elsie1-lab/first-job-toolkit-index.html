Workplace Navigator

Tips for thriving in your first workplace:

  • Communicate clearly with supervisors and colleagues
  • Break tasks into manageable steps and prioritise
  • Ask for help when stuck – it’s a strength, not a weakness
  • Track your progress daily and reflect weekly
  • Learn to accept constructive feedback and adjust
  • Maintain professionalism: punctuality, polite communication, and respect
